Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Everyday LDS is designed to be simple and private. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

Information We Collect

Everyday LDS does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The app does not require users to create an account.

Local Device Data

Everyday LDS may store certain preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Liked or saved verses
  • App theme preference
  • Display settings
  • Name or display preference, if entered by the user

This information remains on your device and is used only to provide app features.

Analytics and Tracking

Everyday LDS does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or cross-app tracking.

Internet Access

Everyday LDS uses scripture content bundled with the app. The app does not require internet access for normal reading features.

Widgets

Everyday LDS may provide Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets. These widgets display scripture content and app information using data available locally on the device.

Data Sharing

Everyday LDS does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Everyday LDS does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information, no personal data from children is stored or shared.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
