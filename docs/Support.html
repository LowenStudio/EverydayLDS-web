Common Questions

Does Everyday LDS require an account?

No. Everyday LDS does not require an account or sign-in.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Everyday LDS uses scripture content bundled with the app, so normal reading features can work without internet access.

Where are liked verses stored?

Liked verses and app preferences are stored locally on your device.

How do I copy a verse?

Press and hold on the verse text to copy it.

How do I save a verse?

Tap the heart button to like and save the current verse.

How do I use the widget?

Add Everyday LDS from the iOS Home Screen or Lock Screen widget gallery. Widget availability may depend on your iOS version and device settings.

Reporting an Issue

When reporting a problem, please include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the issue, if possible
  • A screenshot, if helpful
