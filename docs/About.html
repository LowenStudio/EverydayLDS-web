About Everyday LDS

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Everyday LDS is a simple scripture reading app created to provide a quiet, focused way to read and reflect on Latter-day Saint scripture.

The app is designed around a clean daily reading experience. Users can view scripture verses, move between verses, save favorites, copy verses, and use widgets to keep scripture visible throughout the day.

Purpose

Everyday LDS was built to make scripture reading feel simple, accessible, and present. The goal is not to replace full study tools, but to offer a lightweight companion for daily reflection.

Features

Everyday LDS includes daily scripture viewing, favorite verse saving, long-press copy support, theme settings, and Home Screen and Lock Screen widget support.

Scripture Source

Scripture text used in Everyday LDS is sourced from scriptures.nephi.org.

This source provides copyright-free scripture data. Everyday LDS is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Developer

Everyday LDS is developed by Lowen Studio.

Privacy

Everyday LDS does not require an account and does not collect personal information. App preferences and liked verses are stored locally on the user’s device.
